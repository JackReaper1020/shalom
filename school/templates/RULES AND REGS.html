Rules and Regulations

Shalom English School

General Rules

  1. Attendance: Regular attendance is expected. Students are required to attend classes and arrive on time.
  2. Respect: Students are expected to show respect to teachers, staff, and peers
  3. Bullying: Bullying is not tolerated. Students are expected to treat others with kindness and respect.
  4. Academic Integrity: Plagiarism and cheating are not allowed. Students are expected to do their own work and cite sources properly.
